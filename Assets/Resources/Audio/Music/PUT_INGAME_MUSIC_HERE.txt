Put in-game music files here.

Recommended names:
Music_01.mp3
Music_02.mp3
Music_03.ogg

Supported Unity audio extensions are fine, for example .mp3, .ogg, or .wav.

The runtime Resources folder path is:
Audio/Music

All AudioClips in this folder are loaded as a playlist.
The game uses a randomized shuffle-bag rotation: every track plays once before any track repeats.
When a new rotation begins with 2+ tracks, its first track cannot repeat the final track of the previous rotation.
Mood-tagged tracks are preferred among the tracks still unused in the current rotation, without skipping the rest of the playlist.
